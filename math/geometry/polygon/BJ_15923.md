> Question

* Find perimeter of polygon, with manhattan distance

```txt
Input:
4
10 10
10 40
40 40
40 10

Output:
120
```

{% endtab %}
{% tab title='BJ_15923.py' %}

```py
n=int(input())
L = []
for i in range(n):
  l = list(map(int,input().split()))
  L.append(l)
S = 0
for i in range(n):
  S += abs(L[i][0]-L[i-1][0])
  S += abs(L[i][1]-L[i-1][1])
print(S)
```
