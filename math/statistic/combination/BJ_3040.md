> Question

* Out of 9 numbers, print 7 numbers that sums up to 100

```txt
Input:
7
8
10
13
15
19
20
23
25

Output:
7
8
10
13
19
20
23
```

{% endtab %}
{% tab title='BJ_3040.py' %}

```py
from itertools import*
for n in combinations([int(input()) for _ in [0] * 9], 7):
  if sum(n)==100:
    print(*n)
    break
```
