> Question

* Out of 9 numbers, print 7 numbers that sums up to 100

```txt
Input:
7
8
10
13
15
19
20
23
25

Output:
7
8
10
13
19
20
23
```

{% endtab %}
{% tab title='BJ_2309.py' %}

```py
import itertools
li = [int(input()) for _ in range(9)]
for l in itertools.combinations(li, 7):
  if sum(l) == 100:
    print(*sorted(l), sep='\n')
    break
```
