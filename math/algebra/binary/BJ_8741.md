> Question

* find the sum of all natural numbers with k digits or less, given k, expressed in binary

```txt
Input: 3
Output: 11100
```

{% endtab %}
{% tab title='BJ_8741.py' %}

```py
k = int(input())
print(str(bin((2 ** (k-1)) * ((2 ** k)-1)))[2:])
```
